Our Story

Breaking silence with art
& real conversations

Some artists just need one stage — one opportunity, one community that truly sees them. Petrichor was born from that belief: a safe, creative space where vulnerability meets artistry.

We exist at the intersection of music, art, and mental wellness. From live shows and open mics to creative collaborations and community experiences — this is a space for people who create from the heart.
